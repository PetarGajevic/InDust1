Viking Knitting

Viking knitting is a beautiful, elegant, ancient technique also known as Trichinopoly (chain). Examples of this chain have been found in archeological sites in Scandinavia and the British Isles. Many of these date back to at least 9th and 10th centuries of Vikings times. It seems to have been used to make bracelets, trim on clothes and other decorative items and a cross-over from textiles to metalwork.
